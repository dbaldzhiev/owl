# Professional Icon Prompts for Owl Grasshopper Components

## General Style Guidelines
- **Minimalistic & Flat**: Use clean, bold lines and simple geometric shapes.
- **Color Palette**: 
  - Primary: Black (#000000)
  - Secondary: Orange (#FF9900)
  - Accent/Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- **High Contrast**: Ensure shapes are distinct and legible at 24x24 pixels.
- **Background**: Solid White.
- **File Format**: PNG (24x24 or 48x48 recommended).

---

## Component-Specific Prompts

### 1. Screen Setup (Owl_ScreenSetup)
**Prompt**: A minimalistic icon of a wide cinema screen or a flat rectangular projection surface. Use bold black borders with an orange accent on the screen surface. White background. Professional flat vector style.

### 2. Projector Setup (Owl_ProjectorSetup)
**Prompt**: A minimalistic icon of a digital projector device. Show a simple side profile of the device casting an orange light cone. Black and white device with orange light. Professional flat vector style.

### 3. Audience Setup (Owl_AudienceSetup)
**Prompt**: A minimalistic icon representing an audience. Show a simplified silhouette of a seated person or a small group of three simplified head-and-shoulder silhouettes. Orange accents on the silhouettes. Black lines. Professional flat vector style.

### 4. Railing Setup (Owl_RailingSetup)
**Prompt**: A minimalistic icon of a safety railing. Show two vertical posts connected by a horizontal handrail. Bold black lines with orange highlights on the handrail. Professional flat vector style.

### 5. Stair Setup (Owl_StairSetup)
**Prompt**: A minimalistic icon of physical stairs. Show a profile view of 3-4 steps (stepped line). Bold black lines with an orange highlight on the tread surface. Professional flat vector style.

### 6. Tribune Setup (Owl_TribuneSetup)
**Prompt**: A minimalistic icon of a seating tribune profile. Show a tiered, stepped structure representing many rows. Bold black steps with orange highlights on the vertical riser surfaces. Professional flat vector style.

### 7. Tribune Solver (Owl_TribuneSolver)
**Prompt**: A minimalistic icon for a solver. Show the 'Tribune Setup' stepped profile combined with a small gear or a calculation checkmark symbol in the corner. Black structure with orange gear. Professional flat vector style.

### 8. Analysis (Owl_Analysis)
**Prompt**: A minimalistic icon of an eye (sightline analysis). Show a simple eye silhouette with a single orange dashed line or ray emitting from it. Black eye, orange ray. Professional flat vector style.
